The Series

Four books. Four consciousness models. One underlying reality approached from different angles: real-time system processing, analytical reconstruction, interpretive emotional reconstruction, and internal collapse through weight and meaning.

Real Time Immediate unease without explanation.
Reconstruction Order, evidence, and pattern pressure.
Collapse Meaning carries more weight than event.

Author

G. Curtis Parsons writes cinematic literary psychological thrillers designed to privilege tone, pressure, and aftermath over clean explanation.

The work is built around fracture, implication, and emotional residue rather than overt answers. Each book is intended to stand on its own while contributing to a larger system of perception, rupture, and unease.

This public site is the outward-facing layer of a larger creative system and will continue to expand with book pages, development notes, and future project material.
